The Meditation Process

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I guess the concept allowed me to approach the task with freedom, knowing that I didn't have to have a finished or perfect product by the end of the week. So there was no stress when things didn't quite work the way I wanted, I just carried on and focused more on learning new things.

What did you think about meditation before reading Chapter 2 of Search Inside Yourself?

I've never really done meditation before, but I have read a lot about its benefits and never thought it was a useless thing to do.

What new things have you learned about meditation?

I guess one of the things that I hadn't read so much about was the link between meditation and increased happiness. I think calmness is more the common expectation from meditation, but I hadn't linked that in with happiness as such.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Not really any one in particular. Having not practiced meditation before, I'm open to trying all or any method.

Any other musings?

I really liked the analogy of practicing meditation being like a work out for your attention. The idea that when your attention wavers and you bring it back, that's the moment when it can increase in strength (or focus).
